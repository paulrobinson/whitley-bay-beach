How we pick the best time

We score every hour of the day and find the best 2–3 hour window for a beach walk using four factors:

  • 🌊 Low tide (more sand exposed)
    30%
  • ☀️ Weather (clear > cloudy > rain)
    25%
  • 💨 Low wind speed
    25%
  • 🌡️ Comfortable temperature (~18°C)
    20%
