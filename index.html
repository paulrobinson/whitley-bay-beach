How is the best time chosen?

Each hour is scored using a combination of four factors. The window with the highest average score wins.

  • 🏖 Exposed sand
    30%
  • 🌤 Weather
    25%
  • 💨 Wind speed
    25%
  • 🌡 Temperature
    20%
